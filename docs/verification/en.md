### [1] King, G., Pan, J., & Roberts, M. E. (2017)

**Cited claim**: The Chinese government produces approximately 448 million social media posts per year.

- **Authors**: Gary King (Harvard University), Jennifer Pan (Stanford University), Margaret E. Roberts (UC San Diego)
- **Journal**: *American Political Science Review*, Vol. 111, No. 3, pp. 484–501

- **DOI**: `10.1017/S0003055417000144`
